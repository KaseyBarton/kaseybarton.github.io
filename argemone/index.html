Project Summary

This project examines defense in an apparently well-defended (but understudied) group of plants, the prickly poppies (genus Argemone). There are approximately 32 Argemone species, all of which are native to the Americas (center of diversity in Mexico) with a single endemic species in Hawaiʻi. More...
