Current Lab Members


Kasey E. Barton
Principal Investigator (PI)

{% include email.ssi %}

I joined the Department of Botany faculty at the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa in January 2010. Before that, I was a NERC postdoctoral fellow at the Royal Holloway University of London (collaborating with Julia Koricheva) and received my Ph.D. in Ecology and Evolutionary Biology at the University of Colorado at Boulder (working with Deane Bowers). Prior to entering graduate school, I was a research technician with Sonia Sultan at Wesleyan University. My undergraduate degree is in Biology from Stanford University where I worked with Deborah Gordon and David Ackerly. I have also conducted research in Costa Rica (via OTS) and Finland.

Andrea Westerband
Postdoctoral researcher

Andrea joined the lab in August 2016 and is a plant ecologist that is interested in how plants adapt to resource-limited environments. Her Ph.D. research focused on understanding how photosynthetic rates influence the population dynamics of tropical herbs in the forests of Costa Rica. Her postdoc work focuses on testing functional trait variation in native vs. non-native woody plants in Hawaiian forests.

website: http://acwester.wixsite.com/research
